Kea Sun

Kwaku Darko-Mensah Jnr., better known by his stage name Kae Sun, is a Ghanaian-Canadian singer-songwriter. Kae Sun has presented his music in venues and festivals worldwide both as a solo acoustic act and accompanied by a band. He has also supported such diverse acts as Ana Tijoux, K'naan, Speech Debelle and Janelle Monae
